About the App

Our app helps groups make decisions quickly and fairly. Whether you're choosing where to eat with friends, planning a family trip, or settling roommate debates, we've got you covered with three different decision-making methods.

Regular Vote

Classic democracy. The option with the most votes wins.

Random

Let fate decide! Randomly picks one option from your list.

Ranked Choice

Everyone ranks all options. The choice with the best overall ranking wins.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Regular Vote work?
Each person votes for their favorite option. The option with the most votes wins. It's simple majority voting.
How does Ranked Choice work?
Each participant ranks every option from best to worst (1, 2, 3, etc.). The app adds up all the rankings, and the option with the lowest total score wins. This helps find the choice that works best for everyone, not just the majority.
When should I use Random?
When you truly can't decide or want to be completely fair! Random selection removes all bias and lets fate make the choice for you.
Do all my friends need to download the app?
Not in the current version! One person can create the decision and pass the phone around for everyone to vote. We're working on multi-device support for future updates.
Is my data private?
Yes! We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. All decisions are made locally on your device. Check our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I save my decisions?
The current version focuses on quick, in-the-moment decisions. Decision history and saved templates are features we're considering for future updates based on user feedback.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Please email us at dsimpsonseeds@gmail.com with details about what happened and what device you're using. We appreciate your help making the app better!
