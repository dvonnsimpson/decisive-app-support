Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

The Short Version: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All decisions happen locally on your device.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how our group decision-making app ("the App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Don't Collect

The App is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Decision data (your voting choices, options, or results)
  • Location data
  • Device information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any other personal or identifiable information

How the App Works

All decisions and voting happen entirely on your device. When you:

  • Create a decision with options
  • Vote using any of our three methods (Regular, Ranked, or Random)
  • View results

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or any third parties.

Data Storage

The App may temporarily store decision data locally on your device for the duration of your session. This data is not synced to any cloud service or external server, and is automatically cleared when you close the App or start a new decision.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not contain any code that transmits your data to external parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we introduce features that collect data, we will update this policy and notify users through the App.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over any information used within the App on your device.
